## Calculation Methodology

### Growth Rate
- 5% annual growth rate (UK pension typical)
- Compounded monthly

### Income Projections
- 4% safe withdrawal rate
- 25% tax-free lump sum (up to ¬£268,275 UK limit)
- Remaining pot available for drawdown
